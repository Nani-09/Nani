IMAGE EDITING

Image editing refers to modifying or improving digital or traditional photographic images using different techniques, tools or software. Images produced by scanners, digital cameras or other image-capturing devices may be good, but not perfect. Image editing is done to create the best possible look for the images and also to improve the overall quality of the image according to different parameters.


IMAGE EDITING TOOLS

There are many image editing tools available that can help you modify or improve digital or traditional photographic images. Some of the popular image editing software applications include Adobe Photoshop, Affinity Photo, Pixlr, and Canva. These tools provide advanced image editing operations like data compression, photo organization, and selection of image properties

SOME EXAMPLES OF IMAGE EDITING TOOLS :-

  1. ADOBE PHOTOSHOP :-
    2.

    Adobe photoshop is a powerful image editing software that offers a wide range of tools to enhance and manipulate images. The software has nearly 70 tools that can be used for making selections, cropping and retouching images, adding shapes and type, and many more. Some of the popular tools include the Move tool, Selection tools, Crop tool, Healing Brush tool, Clone Stamp tool, and the Brush tool.


  2. AFFINITY PHOTOSHOP :-
    3.

    Affinity photoshop is a photo editing tool that offers artists and creative professionals tools to manipulate images on a single platform. Directors can use the focus stacking feature to blend high dynamic range (HDR) photos, overlap and stitch panoramic views, and merge several images. It enables designers to execute real-time picture rendering, tweak Photoshop document (PSD) assets, and use studio presets for UI layouts. Its users can also apply filters to single layers or many stacks, add live liquify layers, and see full-resolution previews of modified pictures.


  3. PIXLR :-
    4.

    Pixlr is a cloud-based set of image editing tools and utilities that includes a number of photo editors and a photo sharing service. It offers a wide range of editing tools, including cropping, resizing, rotating, and adjusting the brightness, contrast, saturation, and sharpness of an image. You can also remove blemishes, red eye, and other imperfections, as well as apply filters and effects to your photos
